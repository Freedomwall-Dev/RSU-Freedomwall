About RSU Freedom Wall

RSU Freedom Wall is a digital platform designed for the university community to share thoughts, ideas, and messages in a respectful and creative environment.

Community Guidelines

To maintain a positive environment for all users, we strictly prohibit:

  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Bullying, harassment, or personal attacks
  • Threats or violent content
  • Sexually explicit material
  • False information or impersonation

Violations may result in the removal of content and the suspension or permanent restriction of access to this platform.

How It Works

  1. Click on "Post a Note" to create your message
  2. Write your message (required, max 400 characters)
  3. Optionally add your name (defaults to Anonymous)
  4. Choose a color for your note
  5. Submit and see your note appear on the wall!

Our Mission

We aim to foster:

  • Open and respectful communication
  • Creative expression through colorful notes
  • A safe space for sharing thoughts
  • University community engagement
